Ingredients:

  • 250 gms Pasta
  • Bell pepper
  • Zucchini
  • Olive oil
  • 1 spoon Garlic Ginger paste
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 2 Onions
  • Corns
  • Cheese slice
  • Basic Masalas: 2 spoons of Salt, 1/2 spoon Red chilli powder,1/4 spoon Black pepper, 2 spoons Cornflour, 1 cup Milk

Procedure:

  • Take 4 cups of water and boil pasta in a container for 10 min. and add 2 spoons of oil in it.
  • In a pan add cornflour and stir it until it changes its color to golden-brown and then add milk to it.
  • Stir it continuously till it's consistency is like a cream.
  • In another pan add 2 spoons Olive oil sautee vegetables in it.
  • Add basic masalas,salt, red chilli poweder, black pepper.
  • Add boiled pasta, corns and the cream prepared earlier.
  • U can also add cheese slice to make ur pasta more creamy.

And ur tasty and creamy pasta is ready to be served.

Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup Cocoa powder
  • 1 3/4 Flour
  • 1 1/2 Baking Powder
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • Butter
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable Oil

Procedure:

  • Preheat the oven to 175 degree Celsius.
  • Grease the bowl properly with butter and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  • Sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring just until combined. Stir in the boiling water(the batter eill be thin).
  • Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Cool the cake in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

And ur tasty and delecious cake is all ready to serve ur cravings.
